Attack on Titan

A Tribute to the Fight for Freedom

The Story

In a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction, the last remnants of civilization live behind three concentric walls to protect themselves from giant, man-eating humanoids known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to exterminate the Titans after one of them brings tragedy to his hometown.

Main Characters

Eren Yeager

A young boy who dreams of freedom and joins the Survey Corps to destroy the Titans.

Mikasa Ackermann

Eren's childhood friend, a gifted soldier who will do anything to protect him.

Armin Arlert

Eren's other childhood friend, highly intelligent and a brilliant military strategist.
